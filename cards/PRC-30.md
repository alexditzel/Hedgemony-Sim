Response Card: PRC

Sell US foreign reserves

- Activation: This card can be activated regardless of the turn phase.
- Usage Restrictions:
  - This card can only be used as a response to US player action/pressure against PRC, or if the US player has chosen to openly oppose China in non-mandatory actions.
  - May play once per game
- Cost: 2RP to Play
- Effect: Roll D10
  - 0-4: Status quo
  - 5-8: China minor gain; US -1IP; -1RP
  - 9: China major gain; US -2IP; -1RP
