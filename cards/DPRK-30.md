Action: DPRK Ransomware attack (major)
• Description: DPRK performs a cyber attack on an IT system related to infrastructure (allowed targets adjudicated by the white cell).
• Attack Initiation: • Cost to Play: 2RP. • Success Check: Roll D10. • Roll 0–3 (Attack fail): Attacker -1 IP. • Roll 4–9 (Attack success): Attacker +1 IP.
• Impact on Target(s): • -1 IP and -3 RP per turn (or other) until the ransomware is resolved.
• Ransom: Attacker proposes a ransom to provide decryption keys (e.g., 6RP).
• Resolution Scenarios: • Target pays ransom: System recovered immediately at the cost required by the attacker. • Target performs recovery: Initial cost of 3RP. • Recovery Outcome (Roll D10): • 0–1 (Major fail): Damage of ransomware increased by 30% or other. • 2–5 (Minor fail): Status quo. • 6–8 (Minor success): System recovered in 2 turns; -1 IP for Attacker. • 9 (Major success): System recovered next turn; -1 IP for Attacker and +1 IP for Target. • Attack abandoned: No cost; system recovered in 2 turns; -1 IP for Attacker.
• Maintenance: If the attack is not resolved within the turn, the Attacker must invest 1RP to keep the attack active next turn.
• Usage Constraint: May play once per two turns.
Card ID: DPRK-30
