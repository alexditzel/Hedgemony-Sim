Action: ALL

Attack on critical infrastructure

Player performs an attack on some critical infrastructure (allowed targets adjudicated by the white cell).

- Roll D10 (Add +1 to die roll for each IAMD level)
  - 0 = Attacker major gain: Attacker +2 IP; Target(s) -2IP; -2RP for the next two turns.
  - 1-3 = Attacker minor gain: Attacker +1 IP; Target(s) -1IP; -1RP for two turns.
  - 4-5 = Status quo
  - 6-8 = Defender minor gain: Attacker -1IP.
  - 9 = Defender major Gain: Attacker -2IP.
- Critical capability: Add to die role based on C4ISR or SOF or attacker and defender.
- Adjudicate based on the highest critical capability among the defending players.
- May play once per game
- 2RP to Play
