• Action Name: DPRK Ransomware attack (minor)
• Description: DPRK performs a cyber attack on infrastructure (allowed targets adjudicated by the white cell).
• Initial Roll (D10): • 0-5: Attack fail: Attacker -1 IP • 6-9: Attack success: Attacker +1 IP; Target(s) -1 IP, -3 RP per turn (or other) until ransomware resolved
• Ransom Proposal: Attacker proposes ransom to provide decryption keys (e.g., 6 RP).
• Resolution Scenarios: • Target pays ransom: Cost as required by the attacker; System recovered immediately. • Target performs recovery: Cost of 3 RP - Roll D10: • 0-1 (Major fail): Damage of ransomware increased by 30%, -1 RP, or other. • 2-5 (Minor fail): Status quo. • 6-8 (Minor success): System recovered in 2 turns, -1 IP Attacker. • 9 (Major success): System recovered next turn, -1 IP Attacker, +1 IP Target. • Attack is abandoned: No cost; system recovered in 2 turns, -1 IP Attacker.
• Maintenance: If the attack is not resolved within the turn, the Attacker must invest 1 RP to keep the attack active next turn.
• Play Constraints: • May play once per two turns. • 2 RP to Play.
• Card ID: DPRK-29
