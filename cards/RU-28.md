Action: RUSSIA
Procure weapons from a rogue state

Russia buys weapons from either Iran or North Korea to avoid running out of supplies or to gain an advantage during the war.  
Russia can buy drones from Iran for a price (cost 3RP). Iran gives 1 force factor to Russia. Russia can use this to gain a +1 advantage on the next D10 roll during the war in Ukraine. Russia loses 1 IP.
Russia can buy ammunition from North Korea for a price (cost 3RP). DPRK gives 2 force factors to Russia. Russia can use this to negate the +1 Ukrainian advantage on the next D10 roll during the war in Ukraine. Russia loses 1 IP.
If both are pursued, Russia loses a total of 3IP.
  
At least 2RP to Play  

RU - 28
